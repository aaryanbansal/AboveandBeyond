Ferrari F40

The Ferrari F40 is a mid-engine, rear-wheel drive sports car built from 1987 to 1992, with a total production run of just over 1,300 units. It was designed to celebrate Ferrari's 40th anniversary and was the last car to be personally approved by Enzo Ferrari before his death.

  • Top speed of 201 mph
  • 2-door berlinetta body style
  • 5-speed manual transmission
  • Twin-turbocharged V8 engine with 478 horsepower
  • Weighs just 2,425 pounds
  • Original price of $400,000 USD in 1987
