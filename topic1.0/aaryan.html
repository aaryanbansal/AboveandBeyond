Introduction

IoT, or the Internet of Things, is a network of connected devices and sensors that collect and exchange data over the internet. IoT technology has a wide range of applications across different industries and domains, including smart home automation, industrial IoT, smart agriculture, and smart health.

Smart Home Automation

Smart home automation uses IoT technology to automate and control different devices and appliances in a home. Some common examples of smart home automation include smart thermostats, smart lighting, and smart security systems. Smart home automation can help improve energy efficiency, increase security, and provide more convenience to homeowners.

Industrial IoT

Industrial IoT, or IIoT, uses IoT technology to optimize manufacturing and industrial processes
